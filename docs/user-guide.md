## 1. Getting Started <a name="getting-started"></a>

### Creating Your Account
1. Download the IPX Holding app
2. Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a secure password
5. Verify your email
6. Complete your profile information

### Identity Verification (KYC)
Before you can invest, you must complete identity verification:
1. Go to **Profile** → **Identity Verification**
2. Upload a valid government ID (passport, driver's license)
3. Take a selfie for facial verification
4. Enter your personal details (name, DOB, address)
5. Submit for review (typically 1-2 business days)

## 5. Market & Trading <a name="market-trading"></a>

### Understanding the Market Screen
The Market tab shows real-time trading activity.

**Market Status:**
- Live indicator (green dot pulsing)
- "Open 24/7" - trade anytime

**Market Statistics:**
- 24h Volume: Total trading volume
- Gainers: Properties with positive change
- Losers: Properties with negative change

**Filter Tabs:**
- **All**: All tradeable properties
- **Gainers**: Top performers
- **Losers**: Declining prices

**Property List:**
Each row shows:
- Property image and name
- Current price
- 24h change percentage
- Trading volume

### Viewing Price Charts
1. Tap any property row
2. Price chart appears
3. Select timeframe: 1D, 1W, 1M, 3M, 1Y, ALL
4. Tap "Close" to collapse

### Trading (Buy/Sell)
**To Buy:**
1. Find the property
2. Tap "Buy" button (green)
3. Enter share quantity or dollar amount
4. Choose order type (Market/Limit)
5. Review order details
6. Confirm purchase

**To Sell:**
1. Find the property you own
2. Tap "Sell" button (red)
3. Enter shares to sell
4. Choose order type
5. Review and confirm

### Order Book
- Shows current bids (buy orders)
- Shows current asks (sell orders)
- Helps understand market depth

## 7. Adding Funds <a name="adding-funds"></a>

### How to Add Funds
1. Go to **Portfolio** tab
2. Tap "Add Funds" button
3. Enter amount (or tap quick amounts: $100, $500, $1000, $5000)

### Payment Methods

**Credit/Debit Card (Stripe):**
- 2.9% processing fee
- Instant deposit
- Enter card details securely

**Bank Transfer (Plaid ACH):**
- No fee
- 1-3 business days
- Link your bank account

**Wire Transfer:**
- $25 flat fee
- Same day (domestic) / 2-5 days (international)
- Receive bank instructions

**Apple Pay:**
- 2.9% fee
- Instant
- Use saved cards

**Google Pay:**
- 2.9% fee
- Instant
- Use saved cards

**PayPal:**
- 2.9% fee
- Instant
- Login to PayPal

### Completing Payment
1. Select payment method
2. Enter payment details (if required)
3. Review fee breakdown
4. Tap "Add [Amount]"
5. Wait for confirmation
6. Funds appear in wallet
